Stir fry

What is this dish?

Ask 100 people for their stir fry recipe and you're sure to end up with a minimum of 300 different variations. Basically everyone has their own idea of what a stir fry is, and in that lies the beauty of this dish. You can substitute whatever ingredients you'd like in this classic recipe and chances are it'll be a hit at the table. This particular recipe is jam packed with nutrient rich vegetables and plump shrimp bathed in a savory sauce with just a pinch of heat. You can eat it as is, serve it over plain white rice, or atop your favorite noodles!
